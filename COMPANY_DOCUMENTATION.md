## Company Overview

### Company Identity
**SibloreEvents** is a technology company specializing in event management and ticketing solutions, headquartered in Kenya with operations across East Africa. The company operates as a digital platform connecting event organizers, attendees, merchandise sellers, and affiliate marketers in a comprehensive ecosystem.

### Company Details
```
Company Name: SibloreEvents Limited
Legal Structure: Limited Liability Company
Founded: 2025
Headquarters: Nairobi, Kenya
Primary Market: Kenya and East Africa
Secondary Market: Pan-African expansion target
Industry: Event Technology, SaaS, E-commerce
Employees: 15-25 (Phase 1), 50+ (Phase 2)
Technology Stack: Django, Python, React Native
Platform Type: Web + Mobile Applications
```

### Company Story
Founded in 2025, SibloreEvents emerged from the growing need for a comprehensive event management solution tailored to the African market. The company was established by a team of passionate technologists and event industry professionals who recognized the unique challenges and opportunities in the African events ecosystem.

The name "Siblore" represents the fusion of "Sib" (from the local context meaning connection/community) and "Lore" (representing stories and experiences). This embodies the company's mission to connect communities through memorable experiences.
